# Case Study 01 – Credit Card Customer Analysis

## Overview

This case study analyzes credit card customer data to understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and potential profitability drivers.
The goal of the analysis is to extract insights that could help financial institutions improve customer segmentation, marketing strategies, and risk management.

## Objectives

* Explore customer demographic characteristics
* Analyze credit card usage and spending behavior
* Identify patterns related to credit limits and balances
* Generate business insights that could support decision-making

## Dataset

The dataset includes customer information related to credit card usage.
Key variables include:

* Customer demographics
* Credit limit information
* Balance and transaction data
* Payment behavior

File used in this case study:

```
credit_card_customers_dataset.xlsx
```

## Analysis Approach

The analysis was conducted using spreadsheet-based exploratory analysis techniques.

Main steps include:

1. Data exploration and basic descriptive statistics
2. Identification of key customer characteristics
3. Analysis of spending and credit usage patterns
4. Visualization of important relationships within the data
5. Extraction of business insights

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization

## Key Insights

Some important observations derived from the analysis include:

* Customer spending behavior varies significantly depending on credit limits and usage patterns.
* Certain customer segments demonstrate higher balance levels and potentially higher financial risk.
* Identifying these segments can help financial institutions better target marketing campaigns and manage credit risk.
